MERA ASSIGNMENT

The project is an interactive module between the teachers and the students regarding assignments. The student will upload their assignment with respect to a particular subject and hence the corresponding subject teacher will assist and evaluate the assignment uploaded by the student. It focuses on the idea of effectively using MERA ASSIGNMENT website for a modern day assessment with security and better functionality while the main purpose serves it as a medium for saving paper and making internet it's alternative.
